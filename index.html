Quarterfinal Matchups

Lube Cheeks Vs. OG Lube Cheeks

There comes a moment when friend must turn on friend, brother must turn on brother, lubed cheek on lubed cheek. That moment is now, and I think I speak for everyone when I say: it’s the moment we've all been waiting for.

How poetic for these star crossed lovers to be matched together in the first round of playoffs. Coach Drexel and Coach Gloss have carved out a similar path to playoffs with each of them brandishing a 7-6 win-loss ratio and a cool 1600 points-for. Maybe as a surprise to some, Coach Drexel eked out the seeding advantage by coming from the bottom, as he usually does, to overtaker Gloss in the standings (I called this in week 8).

As of right now, Jason is projected to edge out Gloss by a close difference of 4 points but both teams look little banged up and a single out designation could sway either team from hope to crushing defeat. If I had to pick right now, I’d give slight edge to Juice just because of the devastating Rodgers Adams Stack. Too bad because Josh Jacobs would really seal things here. That being said both teams have some great matchups and are primed for big points. This is a matchup to scout and whoever wins is gonna give Coach Clancy a run for his money in Semi’s. We all have heard about the side action *Gone Sexual* but will we finally get a conclusion to the league's most controversial figures, or will this matchup only bring out more accusations? Probably that one. Good Luck Boys!

McDermonators Vs. Ballin On a Budget

Coach Pritchard has made his triumphant return to the Bool League after a 1 year hiatus in 2019. Some may remember his 2018 roster that terrorized other Boolers and brought Casey to first overall in the regular season and a first round playoff bye. With his appearance this year Ballin on a Budget is not 2 for 2 on making playoffs, but this year he snuck in as the 6th seed. For that he gets to face the 3rd place team: The McDermonators.

Coach Encina has been the second luckiest team in the league, angering some, bewildering many with multiple single digit victories that he was absolutely not deserving of but took with astounding grace and poise. The McDermonators suffered many weeks at the hand of first overall pick Christian McCaffrey but the time has hopefully come for the prodigal Running Back to return to the lineup, Quads permitting. Just in time too seeing as Touchdownio Gibszn just went down with turf toe.

IF we see a return from CMC then this should be a very tight race between Encina and Pritchard. Both teams have excellent stacks at Qb-Wr in Herb-Allen and Russ-Dk, and both stacks are seeing premium matchups against Atlanta and the Jets respectively. Casey’s team is rounded out by Amari Cooper, a healthy David Johnson and Clyde Edwards-NOWHERE while Emilio has been beside himself. Driving around downtown Charlotte begging (thru texts) Matt Rhule to play Christian McCaffrey. Will Casey find fantasy football redemption in 2020? Will Emilio continue his streak of winning every Bool League playoff game he has ever appeared in?

If CMC plays then I have to give the slightest edge to myself on this one, otherwise Casey you got this easy. This matchup holds all the power in Semi Final seeding, If The McDermonators take it then we will see Coach Encina move on to face The Mockeries and the winner of the Cheek Bowl gets Ram Ranchers. If Ballin on a Budget takes it then Casey will be seeing Coach Clancy’s Ram Ranchers and the greatest Cheeks gets to play Kyle.
