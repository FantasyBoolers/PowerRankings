BOOL LEAGUE POWER RANKINGS

WEEK 7

SANDPAPER EDITION

  1. Ram Ranchers-- Clearly, fate has decided for us that the Ram Ranchers are destined to be the first place team this year. It doesn’t matter what decisions Coach Clancy makes, or how good his opponent does. The points will come, every time. I’m not sure who he had to sacrifice to create this hellscape timeline, but this is Ryan’s world, and we’re all living in it.

    Playoff Chances: >99%

  2. The Mockeries-- Coach Kyle is the antithesis of the Ram Ranchers--destiny has worked against him for three straight fantasy seasons now, plaguing his team with injuries every single year. Unlike Coach Clancy, we’ve seen Kyle work hard and make tough choices time and time again to keep his team competitive. For once, you aren’t leeching off the bottom playoff seed, desperately trying to survive. I foresee success in your future.

    Playoff Chances: 86%

  3. Lube Cheeks-- This team has been the source of a controversy that nobody else in the league knows or cares about, but let’s move past the tabloid drama and take a look at this stacked, ranch-dressing powerhouse. Coach Gloss has more top-tier wide-receivers than the Turf Munchers have top tier players in their entire roster. Fun fact: in honor of our bottom two teams in the rankings, Steve got into a literal car wreck this week. Talk about commitment.

    Playoff Chances: 88%

  4. Ballin on a Budget-- It may seem like this team has sleepwalked its way into a playoff spot, even going so far as to literally auto-draft their team, but make no mistake--Coach Pritchard has certainly assembled a contender. With stars such as AJ Green, David Johnson, Mike Evans, and…. Josh Kelley…. And…. I’m gonna stop here before I change my mind about this ranking.

    Playoff Chances: 77%

  5. The Brainiacs-- 9th place in the standings, but 1st in everyone’s heart. After deciding to generously give the rest of the league a 5-game head start, the People’s Champ has returned in full force after assembling the greatest Fantasy Football roster we’ve ever seen. To the other teams fighting desperately for that last playoff spot--prepare to get dick-punched.

    Playoff Chances: 34%

  6. Hotchubb Time Machine-- A true illusionist, Coach Volta has disappeared and re-appeared in the playoff race three or four times already this season. Was last week a sham? Was it real? Are his starting running backs really Boston Scott and La’Mical Perine, or is this some kind of sick joke? Seriously, that wasn’t rhetorical.

    Chances: 38%

  7. OG Lube Cheeks-- This is getting out of hand, now there’s two of them. Coach Drexel was so sure that he was going to come out of this week with a win, that the league has actively put him on suicide watch in the aftermath of a devastating loss. ESPN is reporting that he rejected a one-for-one trade offer of Josh Jacobs for JaMycal Hasty this morning, and when we reached out to him for an interview, all we could make out was slurred, incoherent ramblings about how Coach Gloss stole his team name, and how he can’t wait to make playoffs. Nobody tell him, guys.

    Playoff Chances: 32%

  8. Nobody.-- After looking at the three remaining teams, I came to the conclusion that nobody deserves 8th place. It’s too high for any of these teams, quite frankly. Moving on.

  9. The McDermonators-- If anybody ever looks you in the eye and tells you that there’s no such thing as luck in Fantasy Football, point that person in the direction of the 2020 McDermonators. I’ve never seen a team with more blatant plot armor than this, and quite frankly, it's starting to make me sick. The sad truth is, Coach Encina is going to trip and fall into the playoffs and get absolutely annihilated by the first team that looks in his general direction. We all can’t wait.

    Playoff Chances: 98%

  10. The Alex’s Redskins-- After trading his best player for a sandpaper handjob that Coach Smith bought him on Fiver in the parking lot of a 7/11, Alex laughed victoriously, certain that playoffs were in his future. The rest of the league looked on in disgust, repulsed that Coach Glatz had somehow reached a new low, even for him.

    Playoff Chances: 42%

  11. Turf Munchers-- Do we really have to keep talking about this team? You all read the entry for #10, and how bad that was. Let it sink in that Coach Mungilo has crafted a team that is FAR worse than the next lowest on the power rankings. Just disgusting. It’s time to start looking at next year’s draft, my friend.

    Playoff Chances: 6%
