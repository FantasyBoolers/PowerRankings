THE KING IS BACK EDITION

After a night of steamy, non-consensual lovemaking with Dalvin Cook, Ryan decided he wasn’t going to take anymore disrespect on the power-rankings. He’s gunning for the title.

The best example of this is Coach Mochrie, who got a little too excited and blasted his team into the sun after Saquon Barkley stubbed his toe a few weeks back. If he wasn’t in the finals last year, I’d place him lower--but we all know Kyle has that winner’s mindset, and if anyone can right the ship it’s him.

  6. The Fournetteicatorz -- I spend more time trying to spell the name of your team than I do actually checking to see how you’ve been. All I know is that eventually, mediocre teams will regress to the mean and show their true colors. This team is a good example of that.

  7. Barkley Reincarnated -- Like a phoenix from the ashes, Coach Glatz has managed two straight wins. I once called him a wildcard to win any week, and then I regretted that statement, because his team looked like horseshit. Now, though, I want to go back and un-regret it, because Alex is desperate to make playoffs, and I respect that hunger.

  8. James -- Even though his team loses most of the time, and definitely won’t win the division, and probably won’t make playoffs, and sometimes forgets to set his lineup--Coach Moore has assembled somewhat of a great roster. It’s hard to get anywhere without W’s, though, buddy. I consider this team a dark horse to sneak into playoffs.

  9. Shitbag Titty Fuck -- You won a game, so I’m giving you a freebie bump in the rankings. I’m proud of you, bud. We’re all so proud of you.

  10. Drunk Taylor Swift -- Wow, did you give your boyfriend a pity-win or is your team really this dead in the water? If you’re Taylor Swift, this fantasy league is Kanye West, repeatedly embarrassing you and out-performing you. Be ashamed.
